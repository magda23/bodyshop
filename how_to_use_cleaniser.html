DOUBLE CLEANSING

WHAT IS DOUBLE CLEANSING?

Double cleansing is a two-step method for cleaning your skin: the first step removes make-up and SPF, the second step simply cleanses the skin. Double cleansing may be necessary if you wear a lot of make-up, a daily SPF or spend a lot of time in a city, which can result in added pollution on the skin.

Our Camomile Cleansing Oil and Camomile Sumptuous Cleansing Butter are the perfect duo for leaving skin cleansed, refreshed and nourished.
