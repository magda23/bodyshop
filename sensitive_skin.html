SENSITIVE SKINCARE

LET US GUIDE YOU THROUGH THE BEST FACE WASH, CLEANSER OR MAKEUP REMOVER FOR SENSITIVE SKIN

You need to love your skin and always give it the TLC it needs. Of course, that means the skin on your face, but you should also never ignore your body. You want to feel good, look good, and radiate every day.

What is xx skin?

Our faces are exposed to all sorts on a daily basis. Whether pollutants in the air, the wrong makeup, or the harsh UV rays of the sun, all these things can sometimes make the skin feel a little sensitive.

Why do I get xx skin?

Regularly cleaning the skin on your face is a key part of keeping it looking healthy and free from impurities. It’ll also help you maintain its natural oils. Sometimes using certain products on your face can be hard on your skin, bringing back that dreaded redness and itching. It’s so important to find products that are suitable for sensitive skin and that don’t contain any ingredients that could actually harm your skin.

Shop now

What are the different types of sensitive skin?

NATURALLY SENSITIVE

People with naturally sensitive skin suffer from inflammatory skin conditions such as rosacea, eczema or psoriasis.

ENVIRONMENTALLY SENSITIVE

Over time skin can develop a sensitivity to sun exposure, some skincare products and air pollution.

REACTIVE SKIN

Certain ingredients can trigger reactive symptoms like red, warm and irritated patches.

THIN SKIN

Sun exposure can thin the skin and make veins and capillaries appear more noticeable.

How to treat xx skin?

Facial cleansers are ideal for your everyday skincare routine. Our Aloe Calming Cream Cleanser formulated for sensitive skin has a rich and creamy formula that helps to leave skin feeling gently moisturised.
