Charcoal has become one of the world’s hottest-trending beauty ingredients. But what exactly is this wonder ingredient and why are so many people now adding it to their skincare routine? Discover the benefits of charcoal for your skin.

WHAT IS CHARCOAL?

Charcoal is a dark substance that consists mainly of carbon. It’s produced by heating up wood and plant materials in an oxygen-free environment. Charcoal has been used for its purifying properties for centuries. It’s a microporous material, which means it can help to draw out impurities.

HOW & WHERE IS OUR CHARCOAL SOURCED?

Our bamboo charcoal is farm-grown in the Himalayan foothills. The bamboo is hand-harvested after four years, giving it plenty of time to mature. Bamboo is the fastest growing woody plant in the world, making it a very environmentally friendly resource.

HOW ELSE CAN I PURIFY MY SKIN?

1. Drink plenty of water

Drink at least 2L of water a day to help flush out toxins and keep your body and skin hydrated. We recommend adding fresh lemon and ginger to hot water for a tasty drink that’s infused with nature’s best-loved cleansers.

2. Choose clarifying make-up

Always choose cosmetics that won't clog your pores, like our Matte Clay Skin Clarifying Foundation. Our vegan formula is enriched with Community Trade tea tree oil from Kenya and leaves a breathable, yet full-coverage matte finish that helps skin look clearer.

3. Steam your skin

Steaming your skin is a great way to help purify your pores. Simply fill up a bowl with hot water and place your face over the steam with a towel over your head. Add this step to your skincare routine to help open up pores before masking or cleansing.
